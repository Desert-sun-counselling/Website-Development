Why Desert Sun

Work That Matters

Desert Sun has been a cornerstone of the South Okanagan for over 30 years. Our team combines experience, perspective, and passion to deliver quality programs and resources that make a real difference in our community.

We are a growing organization with 26 programs across Oliver, Osoyoos, and Okanagan Falls. Our staff work across counselling, outreach, crisis support, seniors services, administration, and more — united by a shared commitment to our mission.

Senior Support Workers

Oliver, Osoyoos & OK Falls — Flexible Hours

Are you looking for meaningful, flexible work that fits your schedule? Join our team at Better at Home, a program helping seniors remain independent in their own homes. Our Senior Support Workers provide essential cleaning services while also serving as a friendly, familiar face — a trusted connection who looks out for each senior’s well-being.

Why Work With Us
  • Make a real impact in seniors’ lives
  • Flexible hours that work around your schedule
  • Ideal for parents seeking income during school hours
  • Supportive, compassionate team environment
Duties Include
  • Light housekeeping (vacuuming, dusting, laundry)
  • Friendly conversation and social connection
  • Assistance with daily tasks
  • Observing and reporting changes in well-being
  • Locations: Oliver, Osoyoos, and Okanagan Falls
  • Reliable vehicle and valid driver’s licence required
  • Strong English communication skills essential
  • Compassionate, dependable, and observant
  • Able to work independently in clients’ homes
  • Criminal record check required

Application Process

How to Apply

All applications are submitted by email. We review every application and will be in touch if your experience is a good match for the role.

01

Prepare Your Application

Prepare a current resume and a cover letter that tells us why you are interested in Desert Sun and how your experience relates to the role.

02

Submit by Email

Email your resume and cover letter to admin@desertsun.ca with the position title in the subject line.

03

We Will Be in Touch

We review every application. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview. All applicants must consent to a criminal record check.

General Expression of Interest

Don’t see a current opening that fits? We welcome general expressions of interest. Send us your resume and we will keep it on file for future opportunities.
