Gizella

What holds you back from fully enjoying and experiencing life?

Is it difficult relationships, excessive worries, depression, loss of a loved one, a job or a lifestyle? I offer an empathetic, nonjudgmental, confidential therapeutic space to explore the cause of your unhappiness and find ways to start living a life filled with the outcomes you desire.

My Approach

A Unique-to-You Experience

I draw upon a wide variety of modalities and personal experiences to create a unique-to-you environment and therapeutic experience that provides the self understanding leading to lasting change.

Couples & Relationships

What’s Missing from Your Marriage or Intimate Relationship?

Using the Gottman Institute assessment and sound relationship house model, we can identify the strengths and weaknesses of your relationship. We use research-based interventions focused specifically on your situation and the areas you need improvement to create your ideal relationship.
