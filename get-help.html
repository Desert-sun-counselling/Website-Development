Before You Reach Out

What You Can Expect from Desert Sun

We know that asking for help can feel difficult. Here is what we want you to know.

$0
Always Free

Every Desert Sun program is provided at no cost. You will never be charged for support.

100%
Confidential

What you share with us stays with us. Your privacy is protected at every step.

No
Referral Needed

You can contact us directly. No doctor’s note or agency referral is required to get started.

24/7
Crisis Support

Our crisis line is available around the clock, every day of the year. Help is always a call away.

Immediate Help

Are You in Crisis Right Now?

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call 911 first. Once you are safe, our crisis line connects you to trained support — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. The Women’s Crisis Line is available for women age 16 and over, 8:00 am to 10:00 pm daily.

Call 911 — Emergency Crisis Line: 1 877 723 3911 Women’s Line: (250) 485-7777

What Kind of Help Are You Looking For?

Select what best describes your situation to find the right Desert Sun program for you.

I need to get to safety

Crisis & Shelter

If you are experiencing family violence or abuse and need a safe place to go, Desert Sun can help. Our Cindy Taylor Safe Home provides emergency shelter for women and children, 24 hours a day. Our Youth Shelter supports youth at risk of homelessness.

  • Cindy Taylor Safe Home — women & children fleeing violence
  • Youth Shelter — youth at risk of homelessness
  • Pet-friendly safe home, dry facility
  • Call now: 1 877 723 3911

I want to talk to a counsellor

Free Counselling

Desert Sun offers free individual and group counselling for men, women, children, and youth. Our trauma-informed counsellors provide a safe, confidential space to work through whatever you are facing — at your own pace.

  • Men’s Counselling — email menscounselling@desertsun.ca
  • Women’s Counselling (19+) — email STVoliver2@desertsun.ca
  • Child & Youth Counselling (ages 3–18)
  • Call: (250) 498-2538

Families

Whether you are a new parent, navigating family challenges, or have experienced family violence, Desert Sun has a program to support you. All parenting programs are free, welcoming, and judgment-free.

  • Nobody’s Perfect — for parents of children 0–5
  • Family Support Services — one-on-one in-home support
  • Parenting After Violence — specialized family healing
  • Call: (250) 498-2538

I am a senior needing support

Seniors 60+

Our seniors programs help older adults stay connected, independent, and safe. From transportation to home support to elder abuse prevention, Desert Sun is here for seniors in Oliver, Osoyoos, and Okanagan Falls.

  • Better at Home — non-medical home support services
  • TAPS — Therapeutic Activation Program for Seniors
  • Community Lift — transportation to appointments
  • Call: (250) 498-2538 or (250) 495-6925

Youth

Getting Started

How to Access Support

Reaching out is simple. Here is how to get connected to a Desert Sun program.

01

Call or Email Us

Reach out by phone at (250) 498-2538 (Oliver) or (250) 495-6925 (Osoyoos), or email admin@desertsun.ca. No referral is needed.

02

Tell Us What You Need

A staff member will listen and ask a few questions to understand your situation. You do not need to have all the answers. We will help you figure out the best next step together.

03

Get Connected to Support

We will connect you to the right program — whether that is within Desert Sun or with a community partner. All programs are free. We will walk with you every step of the way.
