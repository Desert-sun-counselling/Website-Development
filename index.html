Programs and Services for Everyone

Whether you need immediate crisis support, counselling, parenting resources, or programs for seniors, Desert Sun is here to help.

Crisis Help

Immediate support when you need it most. Our Safe Home shelter, 24/7 Women's Crisis Line, and Youth Shelter provide confidential care around the clock.

Adult Counselling

Free, confidential counselling for men and women. Self-referrals are accepted for all services, and no waitlist referral is required to get started.

Children and Youth

Counselling and specialized support for children and youth, including our PEACE Program for young people who have experienced family violence.

Who We Are

A community built on care

Desert Sun Counselling and Resource Centre has been a cornerstone of the South Okanagan since 1990. We started small and grew because our community needed us. Today, we offer over 20 programs — all free of charge — to people of all ages in Oliver, Osoyoos, and the surrounding areas.

We are a registered non-profit. Thanks to the generous support of community members and funders, virtually all of our programs are free to access. Self-referrals are welcomed for all services.

“Our work is all about care. Care and believing that tomorrow really can be better than today.” — Marieze Tarr, Executive Director

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by five core values that shape how we show up for every person who walks through our door.

01
Compassion

Motivates us to help others with warmth and genuine care.

02
Integrity

We act in alignment with the values and principles we hold.

03
Empathy

Awareness of and sensitivity to the emotions of others.

04
Equity

We acknowledge differences and provide the support each person needs.

05
