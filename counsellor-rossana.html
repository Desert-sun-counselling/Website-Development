Rossana Garcia-Manzano

Rossana is a compassionate professional providing exemplary care to children and families experiencing life disturbances, helping them develop coping skills to live their lives to the fullest.

Bilingual (English and Spanish) Registered Art Therapist, Registered Counsellor, Yoga Teacher, and Reiki Master with more than 20 years of experience working with adults, youth, and children in different settings.

How I Work

My Approaches

This is a space and opportunity for you to explore, pause, reflect, be curious, and make your own interpretations of what might come up. Our time together is confidential, respectful, and collaborative.

Person-Centred

This space and time is for you and about you. You are important.

Holistic

We are much more than mind, body, or emotions — we are interconnected.

Strength-Based

Your strengths are fundamental. Together we recognize them and work with them.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

What you think matters. CBT helps you notice and reshape patterns of thought.

Play & Expressive Art Therapy

Creative, exploratory, and playful ways to express and process experience.

Mindful Practices

Being in the moment, in the here and now.

Somatic & Body Awareness

Your body is always telling you things — learning to listen is healing.

EMDR

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing — used for trauma and more.

Trauma-Informed

Understanding how trauma lives in the body and mind, and working with care.

Group Psychotherapy

We are not alone. Working in groups helps us understand ourselves better.

Non-Violent Communication

Bringing more awareness to the way you speak to yourself and others.

A Reflection

On Healing

Healing is to let go…

Healing is to release the past; the pain, the sadness…

Healing is forgiveness, forgiving yourself.

Healing is to learn about the circumstances, to learn about your mistakes.

Healing is to grow.

Healing is to see with compassion and care what you have not seen about yourself.

Healing is to want to go within and look beyond the veils of fear and despair, beyond the armor.

Healing is to connect with your inner voice that tells you who you really are.

Healing is to be able to see your own truth, to be your own light.

Healing is to allow — allow the flow of life to take you in its own nature.

— Rossana Garcia-Manzano
