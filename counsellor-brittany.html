Canadian Certified Counsellor (CCC)

Brittany von Burg

Brittany von Burg is a Canadian Certified Counsellor (CCC) recognized by the Canadian Counselling and Psychotherapy Association (CCPA). With a profound commitment to promoting mental wellness, Brittany brings a wealth of experience in supporting individuals across all age groups — children, youth, and adults.

My Approach

Person-Centred & Strengths-Focused

I adopt a person-centred approach, valuing the unique experiences and perspectives of each individual. I believe every person possesses inherent strengths, and my role is to facilitate an environment where those strengths can be recognized and harnessed.

Tailored to You

All Ages

I’ve honed my skills in various therapeutic modalities, allowing me to tailor my approach to the diverse needs of my clients. Whether I’m working with a young child grappling with anxiety, a teenager navigating the complexities of adolescence, or an adult seeking to overcome personal challenges, I remain focused on creating a safe and supportive space for exploration and growth.

Energetic & Connective

Warm Style

My energetic and positive demeanour is infectious, making sessions productive and enjoyable. I connect with clients on a meaningful level, helping them explore their thoughts and feelings in a non-judgmental environment. I’m passionate about fostering resilience and empowering individuals to navigate life’s challenges with confidence.

Who I Work With
